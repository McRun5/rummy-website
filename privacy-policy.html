Introduction

Welcome to Rummy Marroki. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application, available on Google Play and the Apple App Store.

By downloading or using Rummy Marroki, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

Rummy Marroki is a free-to-play card game. We do not charge for any products or services and do not facilitate real-money gambling of any kind.
2

Information We Collect

When you sign in using your Google account, we receive the following information from Google:

  • Display Name (Username) — shown as your in-game name Collected
  • Email Address — used to create and manage your account Collected
  • Google Profile Picture — displayed as your in-game avatar Collected
  • Google User ID — used as your unique account identifier Collected

We also collect limited technical data to operate the App:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App crash and error reports
  • In-game data: scores, virtual coin balance, game history

We do not collect: location, contacts, camera, microphone, financial data, or any other sensitive information.

3

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to operate and improve the App:

  • Create and manage your in-game player account
  • Display your name and avatar within the game
  • Save your game progress and virtual coin balance
  • Enable multiplayer features and friend connections
  • Fix bugs and improve app stability
  • Display advertisements through AppLovin (see Section 5)

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

4

No Tracking or Analytics

We do not use any third-party tracking or analytics tools such as Firebase Analytics, Facebook Analytics, Mixpanel, or similar services.

We do not track your behavior across other apps or websites, and we do not build advertising profiles based on your activity outside of Rummy Marroki.

Your in-app activity stays within Rummy Marroki and is never used to profile you across the web or other apps.
5

Advertising — AppLovin

Rummy Marroki displays ads provided by AppLovin, a third-party advertising network. AppLovin may collect and use certain data to serve ads in accordance with their own privacy policy.

AppLovin may collect: Advertising ID, IP address, and usage data to serve and measure ads.
Their full privacy policy: applovin.com/privacy

You may opt out of personalized ads at any time:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS 14+: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable for Rummy Marroki

We do not control the data AppLovin independently collects. Please review their policy for full details.
